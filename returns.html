Customer Service

Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love every piece. If something isn't right, we'll make it right.

1

Contact Us

Email returns@danedesign.com within 30 days of delivery with your order number and reason for return.

2

We Arrange Collection

For large items, we book a collection at a time convenient to you. No need to find a box — we handle packaging.

3

Refund Processed

Once inspected and received, your refund is issued within 5 business days to your original payment method.

In-Stock Items

In-stock items may be returned within 30 days of delivery provided they are in their original, undamaged condition. Return shipping costs for large items (furniture, lighting) are deducted from your refund at cost price. Small accessories may be returned using the pre-paid label we provide at no charge to you.

Made-to-Order Items

Made-to-order pieces — including any item manufactured in a custom fabric, finish, or dimension — cannot be returned unless they arrive faulty or damaged. If you are uncertain whether a piece is right for your space, we strongly recommend requesting fabric samples and booking a free design consultation before ordering.

Faulty or Damaged Items

If your item arrives damaged or develops a fault within the warranty period, contact us immediately at warranty@danedesign.com with photographs. We will arrange collection and either repair, replace, or refund at no cost to you. All Dane Design furniture carries a 5-year structural warranty.

Exchanges

We are happy to exchange an item for a different size, fabric, or finish where stock permits. Exchanges follow the same 30-day window as returns. Any price difference will be charged or refunded accordingly.
