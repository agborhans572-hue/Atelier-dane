Cookie Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2025

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to site owners. Dane Design A/S ("we", "us") uses cookies on danedesign.com in accordance with this policy and applicable data protection legislation.

Types of cookies we use

Strictly necessary

Required for the site to function. These include session cookies that maintain your shopping basket and login status. They cannot be disabled without affecting site functionality.

Functional

Remember your preferences (language, currency, saved items) to personalise your experience. Enabled by default but can be declined.

Analytics

Help us understand how visitors interact with the site using aggregated, anonymised data (via Plausible Analytics — a privacy-focused, cookieless tool that does not require consent under GDPR).

Marketing

Used only if you have consented, to show relevant advertising on third-party platforms. We do not sell your data to advertisers.

Managing your preferences

You can review and update your cookie preferences at any time by clicking "Cookie Settings" in the site footer. You may also control cookies through your browser settings — see your browser's help documentation for guidance. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality.
