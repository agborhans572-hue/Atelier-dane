Accessibility Statement

Last updated: 1 January 2025

Our commitment

Dane Design A/S is committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to people with disabilities. We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA as published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C).

What we are doing

  • All images include descriptive alt text
  • The site is navigable by keyboard alone
  • Colour contrast ratios meet WCAG AA standards across all pages
  • Interactive elements have visible focus states
  • Forms use properly associated labels
  • Heading hierarchy is logical and consistent
  • We do not use flashing content that could trigger photosensitive conditions

Known limitations

Some older product images may lack detailed alt descriptions. We are auditing and updating these on an ongoing basis. Some third-party embedded content (maps, video) may not fully meet accessibility standards.

Feedback & contact

If you experience any accessibility barriers on our website, or need content provided in an alternative format, please contact us at accessibility@danedesign.com or call +45 12 34 56 78. We aim to respond within 5 business days.

Enforcement

This statement applies to danedesign.com. It was prepared in accordance with the EU Web Accessibility Directive (Directive (EU) 2016/2102). If you are not satisfied with our response, you may contact the Danish Agency for Digital Government (Digitaliseringsstyrelsen).
