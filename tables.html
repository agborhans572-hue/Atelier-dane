The Art of Joinery

Our tables are built to last generations, utilizing traditional wood joinery techniques that eliminate the need for metal fasteners. From precise mortise and tenon joints to elegant dovetails, every connection is a testament to uncompromising craftsmanship and structural integrity.

Mortise & Tenon

Seamless integration for maximum strength in load-bearing frames, ensuring a rigid structure without mechanical hardware.

Dovetail

Interlocking joints that naturally resist pulling apart, providing enduring stability and a beautiful exposed detail.

Bridle Joint

Exposed joinery that celebrates the mechanics of the construction, offering excellent twisting resistance.
