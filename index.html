Our Philosophy

Designed to
last a lifetime.

We believe a chair is not simply a place to sit — it is a quiet companion through the chapters of a life. Everything we make is conceived with this permanence in mind: materials chosen for their honesty, forms resolved for their calm, and every joint shaped by a craftsperson who signs their work.

Since 1987, Dane Design has worked with a small circle of Nordic workshops to create furniture that becomes more itself with age. No trends. No shortcuts. Just considered making.

Materials & Process

Craft in every detail.

We partner with workshops across Scandinavia who share our obsession with material honesty and enduring form.

forest

Solid Hardwoods

We source ash, oak, and walnut exclusively from FSC-certified Scandinavian forests. Each plank is kiln-dried for six months before a craftsperson selects it by hand for figure and grain.

Solid Ash White Oak Walnut
texture

Natural Textiles

Our upholstery fabrics are woven in Danish and Swedish mills practising their craft for generations. We work exclusively with natural fibres — wool, linen, and bouclé — that develop a patina unique to each owner.

Bouclé Wool Pure Linen Alpaca
precision_manufacturing

Joinery & Finish

Every joint is a mortise-and-tenon or hand-cut dovetail — no fasteners that will loosen over time. Surfaces receive three coats of hard-wax oil, applied and buffed by hand between each coat, leaving the wood breathing and alive.

Hand-Joined Hard-Wax Oil Steam-Bent
