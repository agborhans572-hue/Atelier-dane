Orders & Delivery

How long does delivery take? expand_more
Standard items ship within 2–3 weeks. Made-to-order and upholstered pieces take 6–10 weeks from confirmation. You will receive a tracking link and a delivery window once your order leaves the workshop. For large items we use a specialist two-person white-glove delivery service — they will contact you to arrange a convenient time slot.
Do you offer free delivery? expand_more
Free standard delivery on all orders over $500 to mainland addresses. For orders under $500, a flat fee of $45 applies. Remote and island addresses may incur an additional surcharge — you will be notified before confirming your order. Express delivery is available on in-stock items for an additional $95.
Can I change or cancel my order? expand_more
Orders may be amended or cancelled within 48 hours of placement at no charge. After this window, made-to-order items cannot be cancelled as production will have commenced. In-stock items may be cancelled up to the point of dispatch — please email orders@danedesign.com as soon as possible.
Do you ship internationally? expand_more
Yes. We deliver across Europe, North America, and Australia. International duties and taxes are calculated at checkout. For countries not listed, please contact us — we can usually arrange freight on request.

Products & Materials

Can I request a fabric or finish sample? expand_more
Yes — we send fabric and wood finish samples free of charge. Request up to five samples via the product pages or by emailing samples@danedesign.com. Samples are dispatched within 3 business days.
Are custom dimensions available? expand_more
Selected pieces are available in bespoke dimensions — typically our storage, tables, and modular seating. Contact our design team with your requirements and we will advise on feasibility and pricing. Bespoke orders require a 50% deposit and carry a 10–14 week lead time.
Are your woods FSC-certified? expand_more
All timber used in Dane Design products is sourced exclusively from FSC-certified Scandinavian forests. Our sustainability charter, published since 2008, outlines our full material traceability commitments. You can read it on our Sustainability page.

Returns & Warranty

What is your returns policy? expand_more
In-stock items may be returned within 30 days of delivery in original condition. Made-to-order pieces cannot be returned unless faulty. Return shipping for large items is arranged by us and deducted from your refund at cost. See our full Returns & Exchanges page for details.
What does the 5-year warranty cover? expand_more
All Dane Design furniture carries a 5-year guarantee against manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship. This covers structural failure, delamination, and hardware failure under normal domestic use. It does not cover normal wear, surface scratches, or damage resulting from improper care. Lighting products carry a 2-year warranty on electrical components.
