From order to doorstep

Made-to-order means your furniture is built for you. Here is what happens after you check out.

1

Order confirmed

We confirm your order and schedule it into our Copenhagen production calendar.

2

Crafted & inspected

Your piece is made to order and passes a twelve-point quality check by hand.

3

Dispatched

Packed in recycled, recyclable materials and handed to our trusted carrier.

4

Delivered

Standard or white-glove delivery, timed to a window that suits you.

Lead times by region

Shipping times begin once your piece leaves our workshop. Final transit cost is calculated at checkout.

  • United Kingdom1–2 weeks
  • European Union2–3 weeks
  • North America3–4 weeks
  • Rest of world4–6 weeks

Made-to-order pieces add two to six weeks of crafting time before dispatch. We will always share an estimated date in your confirmation email.

Need to arrange a delivery?

Our showroom teams can coordinate timing, building access and assembly for any order, anywhere we ship.
