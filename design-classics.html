The Freya Lounge Chair

Designed by Lars Svensson over four years and twenty-two prototypes, the Freya has been made by hand at Dahl Snedkeri in Copenhagen without interruption for thirty-six years. It was awarded the Danish Design Award in 1989 and inducted into the Danish Design Museum permanent collection in 2001.
