Caring for outdoor teak

Teak needs no treatment to survive outdoors — its natural oils make it weatherproof. If you prefer to maintain the honey colour rather than let it silver naturally, apply teak oil once a year in early spring. Full care instructions are included with every piece. Read our full care guide →
